X Trench Run - Skill Building Game

Students enter a fast-paced, 3D environment where they pilot a spacecraft through a narrow trench filled with obstacles. The game helps students develop reaction time, spatial reasoning, strategic thinking, and resilience as they overcome increasingly difficult challenges.

  • Grade Levels: all ages
  • Skills Developed: spatial awareness, motor skills, reaction time, analyzing patterns
  • Standards Addressed:
    Common Core Mathematical Practices - logical reasoning and structure recognition
  • Classroom Uses:
    Math Integration - analyze the angle or speed required to avoid obstacles, analyze flight paths, and optimize strategies
    Classroom Breaks - improve attention and concentration skills
